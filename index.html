Jerry Seinfeld

American Comedian

Biography


Jerry Seinfeld is an American comedian. He has been in classics like Seinfeld and The Bee Movie. He has been ranked as the 12th greatest stand-up comedian of all time. He has been doing comedy since 1976 and is still doing it. In the special Jerry Before Seinfeld He go's back to The Comic Strip were he first started doing stand-up comedy and it's reception was great with a 93% on Rotten Tomatos. He has accumulated a total of $950 million dollars as his net worth.

Early Life

He was born in Brooklyn, New York on April 29th 1954, and is now 65 years of age. His real name is Jerome Allen Seinfeld. His parents were a Kálmán Seinfeld and Betty Seinfeld. He went to Massapequa High school and later went to Queens College. In 1981 Jerry Seinfeld made a succesful apperance on The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson.
